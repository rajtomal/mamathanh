Who is Mama Thanh? Many years ago, Mama Thanh left her home country of Vietnam to travel abroad, curious and full of drive, with a passion for cooking and knowledge of the specialties of southern Vietnamese cuisine in her luggage. With Mama Thanh in Karlsruhe's Weststadt, she is now fulfilling her long-awaited dream of owning her own restaurant. However, the road was very rocky, whether it was the chef who bailed out just before the deadline or the menus that were delivered too late. That's why Mama Thanh is even more pleased to welcome you to Uhlandstraße in Karlsruhe!

Die Eröffnung

Am 7.März 2019 hat Mama Thanh nach harter Arbeit ihr erstes eigenes Restaurant in Karlsruhe eröffnet. Zur Eröffnung kamen über 500 Gäste, darunter auch bekannte Gesichter aus Karlsruhe.
